Since 2013, UPFOR has advocated for early career and established artists whose work examines and challenges cultural and artistic orthodoxies. We've featured represented and guest artists in over 80 exhibitions in our gallery, via international art fairs, online and in pop-ups, placing work in important collections and museums throughout the world.

As of May 2020, our Portland, Oregon exhibition space closed to allow us to focus on advisory and consulting services for individual and corporate clients. We continue to present an active artistic program online and to collaborate with artists in special projects, publications and art fairs.
